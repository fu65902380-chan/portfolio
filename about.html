About

Siyang 'Chan' Fu is an experienced professional with a deep passion for filmmaking. He continually strives to enhance his technical skills while emphasizing the content and story conveyed through his work. He values open communication with clients and directors, ensuring that the entire team shares a unified vision during production, leading to the successful completion of projects.

With over 7 years of experience in film and photography, Chan has been involved in a diverse range of projects, including short films, web series, commercials, documentaries, and promotional videos. These rich experiences have honed his technical abilities and deepened his understanding of the power of visual storytelling, allowing him to master the art of narrative through imagery.
